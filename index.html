Welcome to the personal website of Antonio Fullone

- Just a regular guy in love with life -

Hello! Welcome to my personal website. My name is Antonio, I am just a regular guy that loves life. I make websites for living. And travel the world whenever I can.

I sometimes work on my unfinished projects, I write about life, love, travel and, sometimes, about my job. I take also very bad photos, but I don’t use filters for it, and I don’t have a personal instagram.

I also read a lot, sometimes I listen on audible or read on my kindle/ Ipad. If you expect to find a geek on this blog you might be disappointed. This is my personal space on the web, you can have a look at my CV or Github profile, but in this website I write about everything. Oh yes, I don’t really use much of social media, but I do have all accounts.

I should say that I am a digital nomad, but I find the term quite limited in my opinion. What I do is that I work remotely and travel as much as I can. At the moment I am based in the Canary Islands, but who knows what comes next :wink:

I am a lot in to meditation and living a spiritual life. I love the nature and I believe is the greatest gift the man has ever had.

I live my life in the glory of love. I love life, the World and the people that live in it.
