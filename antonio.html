Me, myself and I.

24th of September 1977 : I was born in the beautiful, sunny and always smiling south of Italy. I grew up in this place, I remember once I saw the snow and they wrote about it in the newspapers.

24th September 1993 : My life changes forever and will never be the same again. I lost the most important person of my life. It was hard but before to die he teached me to never give up, and so I did. (I'm also a very proud person).

8th of January 2008 : Moved to Barcelona. Started working there in an international environment. I fell in love with Spanish culture, and I could not avoid to become a Barca fan, even my little cat was called Messi :-)

8th of August 211 : Moved to the Netherlands. Lived in Amsterdam, Utrecht and now Delft. I cannot get used to the weather, as you can imagine. Amsterdam is overrated, Utrecht is one of the most beautiful cities in Europe.

Study : I did not go to any university, but I asked a lot of question to Google, he's always been open to help me. Once I learned english I could not stop asking questions anymore, there is no better university, trust me.

Work : front end developer with a big love for Design and Typography. I work as senior front end with Symbaloo.com a website for collecting your favorites links on the web. I am in love with CSS, and in a open relationship with Susy and Sass. Javascript is a good friend of mine, Mootools is cool and Jquery is for losers (I had to say do this, is it written in my contract).

Life, fun, hobbies : I like cooking, travelling and learning new languages. I am trying (with no results) to learn japanese, dutch and german. I do speak spanish, catalan, english and Calabrian. I also play a electric guitar with my Ipad and trained for some years in muay thai. I never watched starwars or lord of the ring, I don't drink red bull and I do most of my job during the day, so in the night I can deal with my sleep disorders. Trying to conduct a healthy life, and make my little nephews happy every time I see them.

Social Life : Sure, twitter is my favorite one, but I also use faceook and G+ (well sort of ..). But honestly I would prefer to meet you in a bar and have a beer together, i find hard to talk with someone in 140 chars, and I don't like to tell all my life to Mr. Zuckerberg.
